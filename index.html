Transforming Particle Physics with AI

NEEDLE is a cutting-edge research initiative developing advanced machine learning systems for analyzing proton-proton collision data from the Large Hadron Collider (LHC). Our project combines state-of-the-art ML techniques with scalable orchestration infrastructure to unlock new insights in particle physics.

By leveraging DAG-based workflow systems and modern ML frameworks, we're building the next generation of tools that will help physicists explore fundamental questions about the universe.
