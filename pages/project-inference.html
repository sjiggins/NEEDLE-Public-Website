Real-Time Inference

Efficient and fast computation of non-equilibrium models

Overview

Non-equilibrium inspired models, such as diffusion/flow-matching, are costly in terms of their inference time. Requiring significant acceleration to be even remotely viable in high energy particle physics data analyses. As such, advanced methodologies and mathematical approximations are required to enable statistical inference with such models.
