Diffusion & Flow-Matching Models

Advanced generative models for likelihood and posterior inference in high-energy physics

Overview

We develop state-of-the-art diffusion and flow-matching models for probabilistic inference in particle physics. These models learn to transform simple prior distributions into complex posterior distributions over physics parameters, enabling efficient sampling and likelihood evaluation for high-dimensional problems.

Model Architectures

Diffusion Models

  • Score-based models: Learning the score function of data distributions
  • DDPM variants: Denoising diffusion probabilistic models optimized for physics
  • Conditional diffusion: Conditioning on detector observables and systematic uncertainties

Flow-Matching

  • Continuous normalizing flows: ODE-based generative models
  • Optimal transport: Flow matching with optimal transport objectives
  • Rectified flows: Straightening probability flows for faster sampling

Physics Applications

Posterior Inference

Our models enable tractable posterior inference for complex physics scenarios where traditional MCMC methods are prohibitively expensive. We achieve orders of magnitude speedup while maintaining statistical accuracy.

Use Cases

  • Parameter estimation from collision events
  • Systematic uncertainty quantification
  • Anomaly detection and model-independent searches
